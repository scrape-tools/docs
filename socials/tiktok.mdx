| Method | Endpoint | Parameters |
|--------|----------|------------|
| GET | `/tiktok/user` | `username` |
| GET | `/tiktok/search` | `query`, `count` (default 10) |
| GET | `/tiktok/video` | `url` |
| GET | `/tiktok/proxy` | `url` (proxies video/download/cover) |
